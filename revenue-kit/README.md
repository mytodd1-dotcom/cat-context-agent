# Revenue Leak Audit — First Revenue Experiment

## Goal

Sell one $249 Revenue Leak Scan within 14 days, then convert a successful client into $299/month weekly monitoring.

## Funnel

1. Pick a dealership or local business with paid traffic or visible lead forms.
2. Run a public, non-destructive three-leak preview.
3. Send one personalized message with one verified finding.
4. Offer the full $249 scan.
5. Deliver the report within 48 hours.
6. Offer the $749 deeper audit or $299/month weekly monitoring after value is proven.

## Daily numbers until the first sale

- 5 businesses reviewed
- 3 personalized messages sent
- 1 free preview completed
- Every reply, meeting, proposal, sale, and dollar recorded

## Stop rule

After 30 qualified prospects, change the headline or niche if there are no replies. After 10 previews, change the offer if there are replies but no paid conversions.
